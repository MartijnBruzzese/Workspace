Overview

This page outlines how I work, what I focus on, and what I deliberately do not offer — so you can quickly see whether there’s a fit.

Not policy documents or marketing language — but practical thinking about interaction flows, safeguards, risk handling, and design decisions that help systems behave responsibly by default.

What I Offer

  • Consent-centered interaction design and user flows
  • Safety-by-architecture (fail-closed behavior, sandboxes, risk classes)
  • Structural protection of vulnerable users
  • Intent-aware system flows and focused friction where it matters
  • Ethical foundations for creative and generative platforms
  • Framework development and early architecture reviews

My approach is grounded in the Universal Consent & Safety Framework (UCSF), with ModelHaven Studio serving as its practical implementation layer.

What You'll Get

I work best in a trusted-advisor role: focused sessions, honest feedback, and clear next steps. This can be a one-off session, or an ongoing mentorship-style track for teams that want to build responsibly.

What I Don't Do

  • Growth hacking
  • Marketing strategy
  • Generic AI optimization
  • Surveillance system design
  • Exploitative engagement models

The work is Care-First, Dignity-Preserving, and Responsibility-Driven.
