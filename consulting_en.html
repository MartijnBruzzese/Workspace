Overview

I provide independent advisory on consent-centered system design, safety architecture, and ethical foundations for AI and digital platforms.

This is not “AI optimization” consulting. The focus is practical: interaction flows, safeguards, risk handling, and design decisions that make systems behave responsibly by default.

What I help with

  • Consent-first interaction design and user flows
  • Safety-by-architecture (fail-closed behavior, sandboxes, risk classes)
  • Structural protection of vulnerable users through safeguards
  • Intent-aware system flows and friction where it matters
  • Ethical foundations for creative and generative platforms
  • Early-stage framework development and architecture reviews

My approach is grounded in the Universal Consent & Safety Framework (UCSF), with ModelHaven Studio serving as its applied implementation layer.

How engagements work

I work best in a “trusted advisor” role: focused sessions, honest feedback, and clear next steps. This can be a one-off advisory call, or an ongoing mentorship-style track for teams building responsibly.

What I do not offer

  • Growth hacking
  • Marketing strategy
  • Generic “AI optimization”
  • Surveillance system design
  • Exploitative engagement models

The work is care-centered, dignity-preserving, and responsibility-driven.
