Overview

I provide independent consulting on consent-centered system design, safety architecture, and ethical infrastructure for AI and digital platforms.

My work focuses on translating ethical principles into operational design choices — not policy documents, but systems that behave responsibly by default.

Scope

This includes:

  • Consent-first interaction design
  • Safety-by-architecture (fail-closed systems, sandboxes, risk classes)
  • Protection of vulnerable subjects through structural safeguards
  • Intent-aware system flows
  • Ethical foundations for creative and generative platforms
  • Framework development for early-stage products and research initiatives

My approach is grounded in the Universal Consent & Safety Framework (UCSF), with ModelHaven Studio serving as its applied implementation layer.

Typical engagements

I work with:

  • early-stage AI startups
  • creative technology teams
  • independent researchers
  • platform designers
  • organizations exploring responsible AI deployment

Engagements range from short advisory sessions to longer-term framework and architecture development.

What I do not offer

I do not provide:

  • growth hacking
  • marketing strategy
  • generic “AI optimization”
  • surveillance system design
  • exploitative engagement models

My work is care-centered, dignity-preserving, and responsibility-driven.
